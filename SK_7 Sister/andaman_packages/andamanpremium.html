Itinerary:

  • DAY 1:Assam(Kaziranga National Park:)

    • Safari to spot rhinos, tigers, and elephants. Evening cultural program featuring local dances.

  • DAY 2:Assam(Majuli Island:)

    • Visit the world’s largest river island.Explore Vaishnavite monasteries (Satras).

  • DAY 3:Assam(Kamakhya Temple & Jorhat:)

    • Morning visit to the sacred Kamakhya Temple in Guwahati. Head to Jorhat for tea garden tours.

  • DAY 4: Meghalaya(Shillong:)

    • Explore Ward’s Lake, Don Bosco Museum, and Shillong Peak.

  • DAY 5: Meghalaya(Cherrapunji:)

    • Visit Nohkalikai Falls, Seven Sisters Falls, and Mawsmai Caves.Experience the Living Root Bridges.

  • DAY 6: Meghalaya(Elephant Falls:)

    • A quick stop on the way to admire this three-tiered waterfall.

  • DAY 7: Arunachal Pradesh(Bomdila:)

    • Explore Bomdila Monastery and enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayas.

  • DAY 8:Kohima:

    • Visit the Kohima War Cemetery and the Naga Heritage Village.

  • DAY 9:Dzukou Valley:

    • A trek to the valley to witness its lush green landscapes and vibrant flowers

  • DAY 10:Loktak Lake:

    • Enjoy the floating islands (phumdis) and boating experience.

  • DAY 11:Imphal:

    • Explore Kangla Fort, Imphal War Cemetery, and Ima Keithel (women’s market).

  • DAY 12:Aizawl:

    • Discover Durtlang Hills and Solomon’s Temple.

  • DAY 13:Reiek:

    • Short trek to Reiek for breathtaking views of the Mizoram countryside.

  • DAY 14:Unakoti:

    • Explore the beautiful palace and museum in Agartala.

  • DAY 15:Ujjayanta Palace:

    • Explore the beautiful palace and museum in Agartala.
