Top Summer Travel Destinations to Explore This Season!

Plan your dream summer getaway with SK Holidays

Munnar, Kerala – The Green Paradise

  • Tea Museum and endless tea gardens
  • Eravikulam National Park – Nilgiri Tahr
  • Kundala Lake boat rides

Munnar's refreshing climate is the perfect summer escape with scenic views and peaceful surroundings.

Manali, Himachal Pradesh – The Himalayan Getaway

  • Rohtang Pass and Solang Valley
  • Hidimba Temple and Old Manali cafés
  • River rafting in Beas River

Enjoy snow-capped mountains, adventure sports, and tranquil moments all summer long.

Andaman & Nicobar Islands – Beach Bliss

  • Scuba diving and snorkeling at Havelock
  • Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show
  • Ross Island ruins and wildlife

Perfect for beach lovers and underwater explorers seeking peace and beauty.

Ooty, Tamil Nadu – Queen of the Hills

  • Ooty Lake and Botanical Gardens
  • Nilgiri Mountain Railway ride
  • Homemade chocolates and tea factories

With its cool breeze and charming landscapes, Ooty is the summer favorite for families.

Coorg, Karnataka – The Scotland of India

  • Abbey Falls and Raja’s Seat
  • Coffee plantation experiences
  • Dubare Elephant Camp

Coorg offers a mix of misty mornings, wildlife, and cozy nature trails.

Travel Tips for Summer

  • Book early to grab the best deals
  • Carry cotton clothes, sunscreen, and stay hydrated
  • Choose eco-tourism for responsible travel
