Top Winter Travel Destinations to Explore This Season!

Plan your magical winter getaway with SK Holidays

Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir – A Snowy Wonderland

  • World-class skiing and snowboarding
  • Gondola rides offering panoramic views
  • Snow-covered pine forests

Perfect for snow lovers and adventure seekers, Gulmarg offers a fairytale winter experience.

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh – Colonial Charm in the Snow

  • Snowfall and scenic hills
  • Mall Road and The Ridge for shopping
  • Jakhoo Temple and heritage architecture

Shimla combines snowy landscapes with colonial elegance for a cozy winter retreat.

Auli, Uttarakhand – The Ski Capital of India

  • Perfect slopes for skiing and snowboarding
  • Views of Nanda Devi and Himalayas
  • Ropeway rides and serene stays

Auli is a must-visit for winter sports and breathtaking mountain scenery.

Leh-Ladakh – Frozen Beauty

  • Frozen lakes like Pangong Tso and Tso Moriri
  • Chadar Trek on the frozen Zanskar River
  • Buddhist monasteries in the snow

For those who crave adventure, Leh in winter is a land of frozen wonder and spiritual calm.

Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh – Eastern Himalayan Escape

  • Snow-covered monasteries
  • Sela Pass and frozen lakes
  • Breathtaking mountain views

Tawang is a hidden gem for those seeking snow, peace, and spiritual enrichment.

Travel Tips for Winter

  • Pack thermal wear, gloves, and moisturizers
  • Check weather conditions and road access
  • Stay hydrated and eat warm food
